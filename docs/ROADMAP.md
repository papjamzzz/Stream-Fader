# StreamFader Roadmap

## Near Term

- **Watch Heat layer** — trending proxy score that surfaces titles gaining momentum, not just overall score
- **"Why this ranked here"** — one-line explanation per card showing which signals pushed it up or down
- **Stronger provider badges** — clearer streaming platform display with logos and direct watch links
- **StreamFinder grounded in candidates** — AI picks constrained to titles already in the ranked feed, not hallucinated
- **Improved Open Graph share cards** — richer previews when sharing a fader position link

## Later

- **Cross-device profiles** — sync watchlist and seen/skipped titles across devices
- **Weekly personalized picks** — email digest of top recommendations based on fader preferences
- **Advanced mood modes** — preset fader + genre configurations for moods (date night, solo, comfort rewatch)
- **Premium tier** — only if a clear premium feature warrants it; no paywalling existing functionality

## Completed

- Critic ↔ audience fader with live resorting
- Movie and TV discovery feeds (6h cache)
- Genre pill filtering
- Watch queue
- Seen It / No Thanks personalization
- Share My Stream
- StreamFinder (Claude / GPT / Gemini)
- Trailer, cast, and actor lookup
- 5i AI Top Pick
- Brevo email capture
- Anonymous analytics
- SEO (Open Graph, robots.txt, sitemap.xml)
- Public beta launch at stream.creativekonsoles.com
