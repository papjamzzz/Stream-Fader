How StreamFader Works

🎚 The Fader
Slide left to rank by critic scores (Rotten Tomatoes, Metacritic). Slide right for audience scores (IMDb, Letterboxd). The middle is the sweet spot where both agree.
SF Score
The number on each card is the StreamFader Score — a blend of critic and audience ratings weighted by where the fader is set. 100 is perfect.
✓ Seen it
Removes the title and boosts similar content in your feed. Dismissed titles return after 30 days.
✗ No thanks
Removes the title and reduces similar content in your feed. Dismissed titles return after 30 days.
The more you use it, the smarter it gets.
